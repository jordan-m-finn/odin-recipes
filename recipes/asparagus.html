How To Make Asparagus

It feels like there's only one way to prepare asparugus... and this is it!

Ingredients You'll NEED

  • Asparagus
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Baking sheet, rack, or anything you can use in an oven

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 farenheight
  2. Aquire baking sheet
  3. Wash and dry asparagus
  4. Lightly coat asparagus with extra virgin olive oil
  5. Apply salt, pepper, and garlic as preferred
  6. Bake asparagus for 15 minutes
  7. Remove and apply lemon
  8. Now you are ready to serve!
